Hardware Setup

Equipment Checklist

Before starting, ensure you have all the necessary components:

  • Raspberry Pi board
  • microSD card (16GB or larger)
  • USB-C power supply
  • HDMI cable
  • USB keyboard
  • USB mouse
  • HDMI-compatible monitor

Connection Steps

  1. Prepare the microSD card (keep it ready but don't insert yet)
  2. Connect the HDMI cable to your monitor and Raspberry Pi
  3. Connect the USB peripherals
  4. Plug in your keyboard
  5. Plug in your mouse
  6. Insert the microSD card
  7. Connect the power supply (always do this last)

Important Safety Notes

  • Always connect the power supply last
  • Use the official power supply when possible
  • Never insert or remove components while powered on
  • Handle the board by its edges to avoid static damage

Next Steps

Once you've completed the hardware setup, proceed to Initial Setup to install the operating system.
